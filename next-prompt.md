Design the Attendance History screen for "Smart Attendance" app.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**

- Primary Accent: Blue
- Backgrounds: Soft White (#F5F8FF), Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- Warning/Error: Red (with soft glow for icons)
- Success: Green
- Font: Inter
- Aesthetics: Light theme, modern, sleek, clean
- NO PHONE FRAME. Screen design only.

**Page Structure:**

- Header with title "Attendance History" and a filter button (calendar icon).
- Overall Stats section: "Total Classes", "Attended", "Absent" with circular progress rings.
- A list of recent classes:
  - History card for each class: Subject Name, Time, and Status (Present/Absent).
  - Use green text for Present and red text for Absent.
